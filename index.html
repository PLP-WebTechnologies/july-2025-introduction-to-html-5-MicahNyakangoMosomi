Smarter Education with EduPlus

EduPlus helps schools and students understand performance patterns using intelligent, data-driven insights. It’s more than a report card—it’s a continuous feedback system.

Key Features

Performance Analytics

Track individual and class performance across subjects, terms, and years.

AI Recommendations

Receive automated tips on how students can improve based on learning patterns.

Progress Contests

Encourage student engagement with friendly competitions that reward growth and consistency.

School Insights

Give educators real-time overviews of class-level performance trends and areas needing support.

How It Works

Students and teachers log in, enter academic records, and the system generates smart feedback using AI. EduPlus turns grades into growth strategies.

Who Can Use EduPlus?

  • Schools and administrators seeking performance visibility
  • Students who want to track progress and improve
  • Teachers aiming to guide their classes with insight
